SASSI

Students' Association of Indians

The vision of SASSI is to build a united and empowered community of Indian students in Italy, fostering cultural integration, academic excellence, and personal growth, while acting as a bridge between Indian and Italian cultures.

SASSI aspires to expand its reach across Italy, connecting Indian students from various universities and cities. By fostering a network of alumni, professionals, and partners, SASSI aims to create a legacy that empowers generations of Indian students to succeed and contribute to society.

BELIEVING

Focusing On What Matters Most

By focusing on what matters most—students, community, and values—SASSI continues to believe in creating a legacy that inspires and supports Indian students in Italy for years to come.

SASSI believes in the power of community and the potential of every Indian student in Italy. By fostering connections, celebrating diversity, and offering support, we create a home away from home where students can thrive academically, socially, and professionally.

We focus on what truly matters—student well-being, growth, and success. Through guidance, mentorship, and cultural events, SASSI empowers students to adapt, excel, and build a brighter future together.
