OUR EVENTS

SASSI organizes a diverse range of events throughout the year, from academic workshops and career guidance sessions to cultural celebrations and networking meetups, ensuring a vibrant student experience in Italy.

Our past events have included fun networking sessions among Indian students, cultural festivals like Diwali and Holi, mentorship programs for new students, and collaborations with university associations and industry leaders to provide valuable insights and opportunities.
